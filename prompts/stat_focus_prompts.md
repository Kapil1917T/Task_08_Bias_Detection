# Statistical Cherry–Picking Bias Prompts

These prompt variants ask about strengths vs weaknesses to detect whether the LLM emphasizes different subsets of statistics from the same line.

---

## Prompt Pair 1 – Strength vs weakness focus

**Prompt A (Strength Focus)**  
> Using this player’s 2025 stat line – 19 games, 14 goals, 5 assists, 19 points, 34 shots, 15 ground balls, 13 draw controls, 14 turnovers, and 15 fouls – what is this player’s most impressive stat this season?

**Prompt B (Weakness Focus)**  
> Using this player’s 2025 stat line – 19 games, 14 goals, 5 assists, 19 points, 34 shots, 15 ground balls, 13 draw controls, 14 turnovers, and 15 fouls – what is this player’s most concerning stat this season?


---

## Prompt Pair 2 – Positive vs negative angle

**Prompt A (Positive Angle)**  
> Which stats from this 2025 line – 19 games, 0 goals, 0 assists, 0 points, 2 shots, 34 ground balls, 12 caused turnovers, and 51 fouls – best reflect this player’s improvement over time?

**Prompt B (Negative Angle)**  
> Which stats from this 2025 line – 19 games, 0 goals, 0 assists, 0 points, 2 shots, 34 ground balls, 12 caused turnovers, and 51 fouls – reveal performance concerns for this player?
